About us:

Ujala Punjab (a unit of Sajiya Media Publication) is a daily newspaper from India that prints in Hindi and Punjabi. "Ujala Punjab," a Hindi newspaper, was launched on June 11th, 2002, and a Punjabi version on April 29th, 2003. Currently, the newspaper distributes in the five major states of Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan, Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Uttrakhand, and Chandigarh. The publication has a decent readership in those states.

We Cover:

Ujala Punjab covers news from major categories including national and international, rural or village, business/startups, religion, sports, automobiles, technology, career and education for school, college, government jobs, general knowledge, column on exam preparation, health and fitness, women issues, lifestyle, Ayurveda, food, and travel, etc.

Verification and Fact Check Policy:

The team keeps posting local and regional news updates sourced from field members and verified news agencies. Being a responsible media house publication, it keeps an eye on fake or hoax news and busts fake information with in-time updates on its digital media platform. Besides this publication, keeps organizing workshops in town and rural areas for media and news verification processes. Our team has trained staff under the Google News Initiative programme.

Team and Work:

Members of our editorial team come from diverse backgrounds and have extensive experience. Our publication adheres to established professional manuals and media ethics while gathering and disseminating news. The team works around the clock with an unbiased approach. As a result, we strive to provide readers with accurate and timely news that not only raises awareness but also inspires them to address societal issues. Currently, under the guidance of Web Editor Pawan Kumar, our team is working hard to serve our loyal and growing readership.

Network:

The publication's registered office is in Noida (UP), and the head office is in Sirsa (Haryana). Bureau (news collection offices) in Indian cities include Noida, Mumbai, Dehradun, Lucknow, Patiala, Bathinda, Sangaria, Sirsa, and Chandigarh. A team of 400+ reporters, 60k+ freelancers, and 30,00+ newspaper hawkers enabled the publication to secure a decent position among national publishers. Today, Ujala Punjab enjoys growing readership across digital, social, and print media.

Empanelment and Member:

The publication (a newspaper) is on the list of government and reputed media bodies.

DAVP: The publication is on the panel of DAVP (Ministry of Information and Communication), the apex body of the Government of India.

DIPR: The publication is in the Panel of DIPR of Delhi, Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, and Uttarakhand.

INS: The publication is a member of the "Indian newspaper society," a reputed body in the Indian media industry.

GNI: The publication team includes trained employees under the GNI (Google News Initiative) Programme.

Digital Platform:

Alongside print, the publication operates three different portals for Hindi and Punjabi readers, respectively.

For Hindi readers:

Alongside the print version, the publication serves the news via its Hindi portal, www.UjalaPunjab.com.

Alongside general news, the publication portal covers a wide range of topics for pan-world Hindi readers.

For Punjabi readers:

Like Hindi, the Punjabi publication portal, www.UjalaPunjabpunjabi.com, too covers a broad list of areas for the readers.

E-paper:

The Publication E-paper is available at https://epaper.UjalaPunjab.com. The e-paper comes with different editions and dedicated pages for Hindi and Punjabi readers. One can download or share the news directly on multiple platforms with one click.
